Ether Community Foundation

Ether community

Through the development concept of "Six Communist Parties" that everyone agrees, everyone creates, everyone builds, everyone governs, everyone shares and everyone wins, all the members who join the ether ecological community are the shareholders of the ether ecological community.

An isolated project may create a product that disrupts the industry, but through collaboration we can create a framework that will change the world.

The relationship between community and ecological development

The goal of community is to unify community strength and expand consensus. The foundation of ecological development lies in the deepening, consolidation and expansion of project consensus. Therefore, community governance and ecological development are interdependent.

(1) Community consensus needs cohesion and expansion of ecological development

Community consensus is dynamic and developing, and ecological development will lead to the deepening and expansion of project consensus. Therefore, community consensus needs the cohesion and expansion of ecological development.

(2) Ecological development needs community consensus promotion and improvement

Eco-development is not a purely spontaneous process, but requires contributions from community members, whether intentional or unintentional. Especially in the case of public chain competition, it needs the integration of community strength and interests.

Community guide

The ether community is built for all

The etheric community welcomes everyone. We defend personal privacy and support personal freedom of speech. Everyone in the community can speak freely, but your speech must respect all members of all ages.

We value your contribution.

As a foundation community, the ether community can only be developed and perfected with the collective efforts of everyone. For us, everyone's participation is invaluable, from innovation advice to community development and management.

We uphold the concept of democracy

We vote on everything to implement democracy. For example, through budget proposals for future development, community votes on brand and communication, etc. We encourage all members of the community to vote democratically on the direction of the project.

We don't set up community rules, we only provide guidelines. Anyone can join, anyone can participate. We voted on these guidelines to guide the project forward.

Focus on the ether community

This community is about the ether community. We encourage exchanges and dialogue with other encrypted currencies, other technologies, and other innovations. Nevertheless, please do not carry out marketing and promotion of other projects on the platform of Ethernet community.

Privacy is vital

Privacy is essential to the community. If a non-individual has made his or her information public, please do not make any act of divulging other people's personal information and human flesh search other users.

Do not make or support inflammatory or aggressive statements, and prohibit harassment and attacks on others to obtain illegal responses. No one shall be personally assaulted, abused or threatened.

Avoiding inappropriate workplace content in public channels

The ether community is suitable for everyone. Some members are under 18 years old and some do not want to read content that is not suitable for the workplace. If you want to share adult content, please communicate through private channels or personal communications.

Stay away from political topics

Everyone has different opinions on political positions. In the ether community, let's create a community that does not involve political issues.

Illegal activities are strictly prohibited

The ether community is a community concerned with privacy and anonymity. Any discussion of illegal activities and illegal acts will be strictly prohibited. All discussions on applications must be limited to the technical level and not to their use.

Respect for each other and resolve differences

Everyone has different opinions about the direction of community development. Everyone's opinion is valid. Divergence is an opportunity. Diversity of opinions can be a driving force for success. Despite different opinions, please maintain an attitude of mutual respect and work together to promote community improvement.
