Introduction of Founder

Ranball C is a technology entrepreneur who has served as CEO of many financial investment institutions. He has experience in business management and providing advice to entrepreneurs. He is also an experienced, high-quality consultant and developer. He is an early investor and an advocate of encrypted currency, focusing on the block chain structure and the chief battle of encrypted currency. Lujia has cooperated with many block chain projects in the world. For more than 10 years, he has been providing technical management consulting, product structure, compliance recommendations and growth strategies for block chains, covering laws, compliance and investor relations, as well as material design, author and marketing.

Mr. Ranball C. Matt, founder of the Ether Community Foundation, was one of the early big miners and one of the core founders of the Ether Foundation team.

Under the influence of ETF, Mr. Ranball C. Matt's ETF was founded in 2014.

In 2014, founder Mr. Ranball C. Matt pointed out that if the financial crisis led to global financial instability and rising unemployment, the ether community would be willing to help more institutions, businesses and organizations benefit.

In 2014, Mr. Ranball C. Matt, founder of the Ether Community Foundation, formally designated the slogan of the Ether Community Foundation as "Increasing Wealth". In the same year, the Ether Community Foundation opened the Ether Community Foundation Cultural Exchange Center in the United States.

In 2015, when the founder, Mr. Ranball C. Matt, opened his office in the UK, the Ether Community Foundation began to expand its territory to the Americas.

In 2016, three shining cultural and financial exchange centers were opened in Frankfurt, Germany, London, England, and Paris, France.

In 2017, the office was officially established in Sydney, Australia.

In 2018, Mr. Mark Benson, Mr. Ranball C. Matt's partner, led more than 20 people to set up an Asian office to formally enter the Asian market.

In the future, the Ether Community Foundation will continue to implement the slogan of "increasing wealth" put forward by Mr. Ranball C. Matt, benefiting from more enterprises, institutions, organizations and ordinary investors under the influence of the Ether Community Foundation.
