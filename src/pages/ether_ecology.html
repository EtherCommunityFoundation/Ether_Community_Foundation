What is etheric ecology?

The etheric ecology refers to the living state of all living things and their interlocking relationship with the environment. For block chain projects, Ethernet ecology is the survival state of various decentralized applications running on the underlying public chain (environment) and the interlocking relationship between them and the underlying public chain (environment). The purpose of public chains (environments) is undoubtedly to develop applications or applications related to block chains, which are commonly referred to as "landing" and ultimately create value through them.

With the advent of the era of Etheric Ecology Promoting Block Chain 3.0, we dedicate intelligent, efficient, large-scale, multi-functional and multi-dimensional Block Chain Ecosphere and application scenarios for the era. And Ethernet ecology not only provides one-stop comprehensive services for the project participants, promoters, merchants, members, exchanges and other organizations involved in the block chain. In addition to the strong ecological layout of block chains, AI, big data, IOT, VR and many other technologies called "Internet Subversion Technology of the New Age" will be introduced in the future, which will pool their existing technical advantages and try to build applications adapted to various scenarios. At the application level, Ethernet ecology will also create an application mode based on block chain technology, which is closely related to the work and life of individuals or institutions, and provide basic technical support for Ethernet ecology. Ethernet ecology will continue to cope with various technological vulnerabilities through the support of multi-chain parallel, modularization and intelligent contracts. The application scenarios do not match, which will build its own application circle for various application scenarios in the future. Continuously evade the ecological disorder caused by technological chaos.
